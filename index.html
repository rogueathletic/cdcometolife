Meet Our Logo

The moment you immerse yourself in hot water, all aspects of your life are impacted. Your body relaxes, your mind calms and your spirit soars. In short, you experience a transformation.

Our logo and tag line were designed to reflect this transformation brought upon by a daily soak.

  • The image is first seen as a stylized butterfly symbol a fitting allusion to the transformative powers of Caldera spas.
  • The butterfly symbol resembles water droplets as they fall and merge with the surface below, and their organic sinuous shapes recall the curved, contoured interiors of our spas.
  • The colors are also significant: on the left, the gray represents the cold, un-awakened state before entering a Caldera spa; on the right, the orange color conveys the warmth and rejuvenation that makes one feel more vibrant and alive after soaking in one of our hot tubs.

Caldera spas are an invitation to a better life, a more joyful and vibrant life. A full life. Come to Caldera. Come to Life.

Sophisticated. Modern. Elegant. The evolution of the Caldera Spas brand is instantly communicated by our new logo. Its organic contours and updated typeface invite customers to take a fresh look at the brand. In addition, the design reinforces our aspirational positioning in the marketplace.
